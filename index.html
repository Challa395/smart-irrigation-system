Smart Irrigation System: Problem Statement

Problem Statement

Monitoring & Management of rate of water, soil fertility, crop growth, pest & diseases, macro & micro-nutrients, etc. using IoT, AI & ML and applications of sensors.

Challenge Description

The technology can help improve efficiency of water usage in agriculture and finds application in open farms, greenhouses, gardening, and research and agricultural labs. It can help with guidance about ways to optimize water usage as per the requirement of crops and soil.

In case of limited water resources, farmers can detect and limit watering to only a critical phase of the crop cycle, thus not affecting overall yield.

Expected Outcome

Development of an indigenous system to track the water, soil fertility, crop growth, and other parameters using IoT, AI & ML and sensors to improve agricultural efficiency.
